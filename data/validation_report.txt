[CROSS-001] INFO - Frankish Empire and Carolingian Empire overlap
  Entities: Regnum Francorum (481-843) and Imperium Carolingicum (800-888)
  Issue: These two entities overlap temporally (800-843). This is
  historically accurate -- the Carolingian Empire was the Frankish Empire
  under Carolingian rule, and the 843 Treaty of Verdun split both. However,
  the data could clarify the relationship between these entities.
  Suggested fix: Add cross-references between the two entities, or merge
  into a single entity with phase documentation.

[CROSS-002] INFO - Roman Empire and Byzantine Empire continuity
  Entities: Imperium Romanum (-27 to 476) and Basileia Romaion (395-1453)
  Issue: These overlap from 395-476. The Byzantine Empire considered itself
  the Roman Empire. The 395 start date for Byzantium is a modern convention
  (permanent East-West division). The ethical_notes handle this well.
  Suggested fix: No change needed, but could add a cross-reference.

[CROSS-003] MODERATE - British Raj and Mughal Empire temporal gap
  Entities: Mughal Empire (1526-1857) and British Raj (1858-1947)
  Issue: The Mughal Empire ends in 1857 and the British Raj starts in 1858.
  This is historically correct (the Raj was formally established after
  the 1857 rebellion ended the Mughal facade), but the EIC period
  (1757-1857) is not represented as a separate entity.
  Suggested fix: Consider adding an East India Company entity for
  1757-1858, or note this gap in documentation.

[CROSS-004] INFO - Kingdom of Castile and Crown of Aragon merger
  Entities: Reino de Castilla (1065-1516) and Corona d'Arago (1162-1716)
  Issue: Both entities exist but there is no entity for unified Spain
  post-1516 (or post-1479 personal union). The Crown of Aragon extends
  to 1716 (Nueva Planta) while Castile ends at 1516 (accession of
  Charles V). This gap in post-unification Spain could be confusing.
  Suggested fix: Consider adding a unified Spanish Monarchy entity
  (1516-1700 or 1479-present).

[CROSS-005] MODERATE - Chimu conquest documented from both sides
  Entities: Chimor (batch_04, ends 1470) and Tawantinsuyu (batch_00)
  Issue: The Chimu entity documents its conquest by the Inca in 1470,
  but the Tawantinsuyu entity does not document the conquest of Chimor.
  This is an asymmetry in cross-entity documentation.
  Suggested fix: Add the Chimor conquest to Tawantinsuyu's
  territory_changes. See also TC-001.

[CROSS-006] INFO - Palestine/Israel in batch_00 vs. State of Israel in batch_05
  Entities: "فلسطين / ישראל" (batch_00, disputed_territory) and
  "מדינת ישראל" (batch_05, republic)

  Suggested fix: Add cross-references between the two entities.
